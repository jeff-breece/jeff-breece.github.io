Since it can’t always be about Christmas Dinner, today included a hefty workout and a vegetable filled recovery drink.

Ingredients

  * 1 beet
  * 1 banana
  * 2 tbsp black Chia seeds
  * 2 scoops French vanilla whey protein
  * filtered water.



Process

  1. Blend until smooth throwing in a few ice cubes and down the mix.
