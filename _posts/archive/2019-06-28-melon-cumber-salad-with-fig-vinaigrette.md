This was a lovely little something fresh to go with our Easter dinner yesterday. Great cheese, homemade bread, dessert with a humble story to come and, of course, a honey glazed spiral cut smoked ham. Back to the salad though…

Ingredients

  * 2 English cucumbers
  * 1 Musk melon
  * Zest of 1 lemon
  * 1 part fig vinegar
  * 1 part balsamic vinegar
  * 2 parts really good olive oil
  * 2 tbsp. lemon juice
  * Goat cheese
  * Mint



Process

  1. Trim the skin of the English cucumbers and slice into ¼ inch rounds.
  2. Using a scoop, ball a musk melon.
  3. Add lemon zest.
  4. Drizzle the dressing all around and toss gently.
  5. Crumble goat cheese all around and garnish with chopped mint.
