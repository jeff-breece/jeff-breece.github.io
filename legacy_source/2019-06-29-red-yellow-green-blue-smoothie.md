# Red, Yellow, Green & Blue Smoothie

__Posted on[ November 6, 2014](https://web.archive.org/web/20190629015928/https://jeffbreece.com/red-yellow-green-blue-smoothie/ "Red, Yellow, Green & Blue Smoothie") by [![](https://web.archive.org/web/20190629015928im_/https://secure.gravatar.com/avatar/e0ec49ea47c3c9e70f0f0098fd1a4d12?s=32&d=mm&r=g)Jeff Breece](https://web.archive.org/web/20190629015928/https://jeffbreece.com/author/jeff/ "View all posts by Jeff Breece") Posted in [food](https://web.archive.org/web/20190629015928/https://jeffbreece.com/category/food/) — [No Comments ↓](https://web.archive.org/web/20190629015928/https://jeffbreece.com/red-yellow-green-blue-smoothie/#respond)

This was a fantastic blended green drink.  


Ingredients  


  * 2 beets, 5 big kale leaves  

  * 1/2 lbs. of baby spinach  

  * 1 cup blueberries  

  * 1/2 cup mango  

  * 1 pear  

  * 1/4 cup cacao nibs  

  * Unsweetened almond milk  




Process  


  1. Run it all through a blender until the texture is very smooth.
