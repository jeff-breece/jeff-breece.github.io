# Beet & Chia Smoothie

__Posted on[ December 30, 2014](https://web.archive.org/web/20190828162719/https://jeffbreece.com/beet-chia-smoothie/ "Beet & Chia Smoothie") by [![](https://web.archive.org/web/20190828162719im_/https://secure.gravatar.com/avatar/e0ec49ea47c3c9e70f0f0098fd1a4d12?s=32&d=mm&r=g)Jeff Breece](https://web.archive.org/web/20190828162719/https://jeffbreece.com/author/jeff/ "View all posts by Jeff Breece") Posted in [food](https://web.archive.org/web/20190828162719/https://jeffbreece.com/category/food/) — [No Comments ↓](https://web.archive.org/web/20190828162719/https://jeffbreece.com/beet-chia-smoothie/#respond)

Since it can’t always be about Christmas Dinner, today included a hefty workout and a vegetable filled recovery drink.

Ingredients

  * 1 beet
  * 1 banana
  * 2 tbsp black Chia seeds
  * 2 scoops French vanilla whey protein
  * filtered water.



Process

  1. Blend until smooth throwing in a few ice cubes and down the mix.
