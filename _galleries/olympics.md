Old growth rain forest, coastal fog that never quite burned off, ridgelines that earned their names. This is what six days on the Olympic Peninsula looked like through a Leica.

The shots are organized by day and shooting session — six banks, roughly 50 frames each. No heavy editing. These are field notes in JPEG form.
